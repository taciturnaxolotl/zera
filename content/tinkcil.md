Tinkcil is an app that lets you control your ironOS soldering iron from your phone all in a gorgeous native app.

![app screenshot](https://l4.dunkirk.sh/i/UZsOAhnWqx8h.webp){caption="the app even supports iPad!"}

### privacy policy

**We do not collect any personal data.**

This app is entirely local and between your phone and your soldering iron. There is no external communication happening at any time.

### support

Having issues? Email me at `kieran@dunkirk.sh` with questions, bug reports, or feature requests. If you are curious the app is open source over at [tangled.org/dunkirk.sh/tinkcil](https://tangled.org/dunkirk.sh/tinkcil)
