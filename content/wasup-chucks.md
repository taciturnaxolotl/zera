Wasup Chuck's is an iOS app that displays Cedarville University dining hall menus in beautiful, glanceable widgets.

!![app screenshot](https://l4.dunkirk.sh/i/d55T6e4ts3Pj.webp)[widget screenshot](https://l4.dunkirk.sh/i/Wyof-1AwkOBN.webp){caption="a few screenshots of the app"}

### privacy policy

**We do not collect any personal data.**

The app fetches menu data directly from Cedarville University's public dining API (`diningdata.cedarville.edu`). This data is cached locally on your device to enable widget functionality. No data is sent to external servers or shared with third parties.
